    Why MoviPlayer?

    Built for the files other players refuse to touch. Play anything from MKV to HEVC to AV1 — right in your browser, with nothing to download or install.

    Universal Formats

    MKV, AVI, HEVC, AV1, VP9, HDR — MoviPlayer handles video formats that your browser normally can't play. No codec packs, no plugins, no conversions needed. Just drop any video file and it plays instantly.

    100% Private

    Your videos never leave your device. Everything runs locally in your browser with zero uploads, no servers, and no tracking. Your media stays private — always.

    Subtitles & Multi-Audio

    Full support for embedded subtitles (SRT, ASS, SSA), multiple audio tracks, and soft-sub rendering. Switch between languages and subtitle tracks without any extra tools or software.

    WebCodecs Powered

    Hardware-accelerated video decoding via the WebCodecs API delivers smooth, stutter-free playback even on large 4K files. No software decoding overhead — your GPU does the heavy lifting.

    How It Works

    Playing any video in your browser takes just three simple steps. No sign-up, no software, no waiting.

    1. Drop or Paste

    Drag and drop a video file anywhere on the page, use the file picker to browse your device, or paste a direct video URL to stream remotely.

    2. It Just Plays

    MoviPlayer automatically detects the format, codec, and container. Whether it's an MKV with HEVC, an AVI with legacy codecs, or a 4K HDR file — playback starts immediately.

    3. Full Control

    Enjoy a full-featured player with keyboard shortcuts, playback speed control, picture-in-picture, fullscreen, volume normalization, and seek thumbnails — all built in.

    Supported Video Formats

    MoviPlayer uses FFmpeg WebAssembly for demuxing and the WebCodecs API for hardware-accelerated decoding. This means you can play anything — from old AVI files to modern 4K HDR content — without downloading any software.

    Supported containers include MKV, MP4, AVI, FLV, MOV, MPEG-TS, and WebM. Supported codecs include H.264, H.265 (HEVC), AV1, VP8, VP9, and AAC/Opus/FLAC audio. HDR10 and HLG high dynamic range content is also supported on compatible displays.
